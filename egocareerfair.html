About the E.G.O. Career Fair

The E.G.O. Career Fair is designed to connect students with top employers in engineering, technology, and related industries. Our goal is to empower underrepresented minorities and provide them with career opportunities and mentorship.

Details

  • Date: October 29, 2024
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
  • Location: San Diego State University, Montezuma Hall (2nd Floor of Student Union)
