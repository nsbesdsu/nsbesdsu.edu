What is the E.G.O. Career Fair?

The Engineering, Growth, and Opportunity (E.G.O.) Career Fair is an annual event hosted by NSBE - SDSU. It serves as a platform for students to connect with top-tier companies and industry leaders, providing unique opportunities for networking, internships, and full-time positions.
