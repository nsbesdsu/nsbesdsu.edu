Why Become a Member?

By joining the National Society of Black Engineers at SDSU, you become part of a strong and supportive community that offers invaluable resources, networking opportunities, and personal growth. Members benefit from:

  • Exclusive access to professional development events
  • Mentorship and leadership opportunities
  • Networking with industry professionals
  • Opportunities to attend NSBE regional and national conferences
