Why Sponsor Us?

By sponsoring the National Society of Black Engineers at SDSU, your organization will support future leaders in the engineering and STEM fields. Your sponsorship will enable us to:

  • Provide scholarships to students
  • Host professional development workshops
  • Fund travel for regional and national NSBE conferences
  • Promote STEM education and diversity initiatives

Sponsorship Tiers

We offer various sponsorship tiers with unique benefits for your organization:

  • Gold Tier - Exclusive event sponsorship, logo on all promotional materials
  • Silver Tier - Logo on website and promotional materials
  • Bronze Tier - Recognition in event programs

For more information on how to become a sponsor, please contact us at contact@nsbesdsu.org.
