Who We Are

The National Society of Black Engineers at San Diego State University is part of a global organization that seeks to promote and encourage the success of Black engineers across the world. Our chapter focuses on providing support, resources, and opportunities for our members to thrive both academically and professionally.

Our History

Since our founding, the SDSU Chapter of NSBE has been committed to helping students succeed. We take pride in our vibrant community of future engineers and professionals who work together to uplift and empower one another.

Our Values

  • Excellence in engineering and academics
  • Fostering a strong, supportive community
  • Encouraging leadership and professional development
  • Positively impacting our community
