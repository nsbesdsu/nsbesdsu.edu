About National Society of Black Engineers (SDSU)

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) SDSU chapter is committed to fostering academic excellence and promoting the inclusion of Black engineers in STEM fields. Our mission is to help members excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community.
